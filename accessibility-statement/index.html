Accessibility Statement for Ask Good Questions

This is an accessibility statement from Wiktoria Van Harneveldt.

Measures to support accessibility

Wiktoria Van Harneveldt takes the following measures to ensure accessibility of Ask Good Questions:

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission statement.
  • Include accessibility throughout our internal policies.
  • Integrate accessibility into our procurement practices.
  • Assign clear accessibility goals and responsibilities.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) defines requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. It defines three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. Ask Good Questions is fully conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. Fully conformant means that the content fully conforms to the accessibility standard without any exceptions.
